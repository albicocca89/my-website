About Me

I received a BEng and MSc in Energy and Nuclear Engineering from Politecnico di Torino, Italy. My academic path has been driven by a strong interest in energy systems and applied physics.

Research Interests

My research focuses on fluid flow modeling and regulation, with applications in both biomedical systems and mechanical/energy systems. I am particularly interested in the interaction between fluid dynamics and system control in complex environments.
