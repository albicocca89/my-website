About Me

I am currently a Senior Lecturer within the Mechanical Engineering Department and Zienkiewicz Institute for Modelling, Data and AI at Swansea University, UK. Before this appointment, I received a BEng and MSc in Energy and Nuclear Engineering from Politecnico di Torino, Italy (2012, 2014) and a PhD in Cardiovascular Modeling from the Wales Heart Research Insitute at Cardiff University, UK (2018).

Research Interests

My research focuses on fluid flow modeling and regulation, with applications in both biomedical systems and mechanical/energy systems. I am particularly interested in the interaction between fluid dynamics and system control in complex environments.
